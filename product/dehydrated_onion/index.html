Dehydrated Onion

Dehydrated onions are a convenient and longlasting ingredient, retaining the flavor and nutrients of fresh onions while offering easy storage and use in various forms like flakes, powder and granules. They are widely used in soups, sauces, seasonings and ready to eat meals. In India, onions are predominantly grown in states like Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Bihar and Uttar Pradesh. These regions not only meet local demand but also contribute significantly to the global supply of both fresh and dehydrated onions. We process onion into flakes, chopped 3-5 mm, minced 1-3 mm, granules 0.5-1 mm and even powder form
