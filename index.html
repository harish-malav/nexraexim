Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Indian spices famous worldwide?

India is renowned for being the land of spices for how they are sourced from varied regions like tropical forests, highlands, marshy woods, lush green lands and fertile valleys. The country's diverse communities use exotic indian spices to create delectable cuisines further.

How can I know the reputable spices suppliers in India?

Always look for the quality standards, certifications, and delivery methods used by the suppliers. Moreover, to identify a reputable supplier, you can have a client experience with them.

What certifications do you hold?

Our products are certified for quality and safety, meeting all international standards. We have achieved several certifications that ensure our commitment to excellence

What makes Nexra Exim trustworthy and outstanding?

Our products' quality, rellability, freshness, and purity make us trustworthy and outstanding in the market.

How do you maintain quality during shipping?

We use airtight and moisture-proof packaging with proper sealing and inner liners. All products undergo quality checks before dispatch, and our logistics partners ensure safe transit with controlled handling and temperature

What should I check before choosing an Indian spices exporter?

When selecting a supplier, look for:

  • Verified certifications
  • Quality testing capability
  • Transparent sourcing
  • Flexible packaging & shipping options
  • Export documentation support

What are the government regulations about the export of Indian spices?

  • Business registration
  • IEC (Importer Exporter Code)
  • The authorized Dealer code (AD Code)
  • Spice registration certificate as an exporter of spices (CRES)
